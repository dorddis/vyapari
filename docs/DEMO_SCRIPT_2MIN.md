# Vyapari Agent - 2 Minute Demo Script

## Goal

This demo should make judges feel that Vyapari Agent is not just a chatbot. It:

- onboards a business in minutes through chat
- understands customer intent
- gives honest, personalized recommendations
- recovers budget-sensitive leads intelligently
- gives the owner business analytics through chat
- updates inventory state from real-world proof
- continues the conversation smoothly after human handoff

## Demo Format

- Two screens visible
- Left screen: Customer chat
- Right screen: Owner chat
- One narrator + one driver
- Total duration: about 2 minutes

## Demo Setup

Use these background elements before recording:

- Business: `Sharma Motors`
- City: `Mumbai`
- Owner: `Rajesh`
- Customer: `Aman`
- Lead source: `Creta reel`
- Highlight car 1: `2022 Tata Nexon XZ+`
- Highlight car 2: `2021 Maruti Brezza VXi`
- Nexon status at start: `Available`
- Nexon status after token screenshot: `Reserved`

Seed the demo state so the owner analytics can respond cleanly:

- `12 leads today`
- `4 leads under 5 lakh`
- `5 leads in 6 to 9 lakh`
- `3 leads above 9 lakh`
- `Nexon` and `Brezza` are the most asked-about cars in the mid-range SUV segment

## Core Product Behaviors To Show

1. The owner can onboard the business through chat in minutes.
2. The agent mirrors the user's language naturally.
3. The agent gives truthful recommendations, not generic sales talk.
4. The agent notices budget disappointment and recovers the lead with finance/insurance options.
5. The owner can ask for useful analytics in natural language.
6. The owner can take over at the right moment without friction.
7. A token screenshot updates inventory status immediately.
8. After the handoff, the agent still understands context and completes follow-up actions like sending the address.

## Agent Tone Rules

- Reply in the same language style as the user
- Keep responses short and WhatsApp-like
- Sound like a smart dealership salesperson, not a bot
- Be honest about trade-offs
- Never invent discounts or inventory
- Use finance and insurance as recovery tools when budget stress is detected

## Why This Demo Works

- It shows instant owner onboarding through chat
- It shows recommendation intelligence
- It shows financial recovery intelligence
- It shows owner analytics intelligence
- It shows human handoff at the right moment
- It shows inventory automation from token proof
- It shows context continuity after handoff

## If Time Runs Short

Cut in this order:

1. Shorten the owner onboarding narration, but keep the onboarding flow itself.
2. Shorten the owner analytics explanation to one message instead of three.
3. Shorten the finance explanation to one message.
4. Keep the token screenshot and address follow-through at all costs because they create the strongest finish.
